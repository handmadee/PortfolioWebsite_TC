### 1. Portrait vs Circle

- Shows more of person (head to shoulders)

**Decision:** 420x520px (from 320x320px)
**Reason:**
- Center piece of portfolio
- Face recognition importance

- Visual balance
- Portrait gets deserved space
- Responsive works better
